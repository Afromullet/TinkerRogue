# Threat Maps

Use multiple layers of threat maps


# Context Snapshot

Much of this can be captured in different kind of threat maps

**Board State**

- Threat Map

**Combat State**

- Expected Damage Dealt
- Change to Kill
- Enemy Danger Level (HP, Number of Units in Squad)
- Vulnerable or High Value Targets (Healers, low squad size)
- Projected Damage (If I move here, what damage could It take)
- Enemy Ranges


**Survivability**

- Expected Damage Taken
- Change of Death
- Exposure to Enemies
- HP
- Unit Roles

**Positioning**

- Terrain Bonuses
- Zone Control
- Blocking Choke Points
- Objectives

**Squad Specific**

- Matchup
